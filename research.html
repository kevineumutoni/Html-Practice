Kevine's summary of what she learnt in UX Research

In UX research we learnt that user experience research, is a systematic study of how users interact with a product or service. It's used to design products and services that meet user needs. we learnt that they are different methods of doing research which includes:

  • Qualitative research: It involves data not in numbers
  • Quantitative research: It involves numbers
  • Primary research: It is done on the field interacting directly with users
  • Secondary research: It is collected from the previous research data.
